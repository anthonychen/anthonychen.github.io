OpenClaw – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2025

OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant operated by Anthony Chen ("we," "us," or "our"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with text messages sent through OpenClaw.

Information We Collect

When you receive messages from OpenClaw, we may store your mobile phone number and message history solely for the purpose of delivering notifications, reminders, and informational messages on behalf of the account holder.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to send you relevant messages as coordinated by the account holder. This includes calendar reminders, reservation confirmations, delivery alerts, task notifications, and coordination messages.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose your mobile phone number or any personal information to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes at any time.

Data Retention

Message logs and phone numbers are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and are deleted upon request.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, reply HELP.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us by replying HELP to any message from OpenClaw.

OpenClaw – Terms & Conditions

Last updated: February 7, 2025

Program Name

OpenClaw Personal AI Assistant Messaging Service

Program Description

OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant that sends low-volume notifications, reminders, and informational messages to the account holder and their household. Messages include calendar reminders, reservation confirmations, delivery alerts, task notifications, and occasional coordination messages to family members, friends, or service providers on behalf of the account holder.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to approximately 10 messages per week, though volume is typically lower.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please consult your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-In

By providing your phone number to the account holder and agreeing to receive messages from OpenClaw, you consent to receive text messages from this service. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

Opt-Out

You can cancel messages at any time by texting STOP to any message from OpenClaw. After sending STOP, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and will no longer receive messages from this service.

Help

For support, text HELP to any message from OpenClaw. You may also contact the account holder directly.

Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Please refer to our Privacy Policy above. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
